Our story

The kind of place
you keep coming back to.

Coast is a neighbourhood cafe — the real kind. The sort where the staff remember how you take your coffee, where kids are welcome and the prams fit through the door, where you can sit outside and feel the morning air off Saltwater Promenade.

We've been part of the Saltwater Coast community for years. Walk-ins welcome, locals encouraged, visitors made to feel at home.
