What we offer

Everything your business needs, included.

No hidden costs, no fitout headaches. Move in ready from day one.

Serviced Offices

Private, fully furnished offices for 1–12 people. Floor-to-ceiling windows, air conditioning, and business-grade ethernet. Available short or long term.

Boardroom & Meeting Rooms

Impress your clients in a professional boardroom overlooking Point Cook Town Centre. Audio-visual equipment and catering available on request.

Reception Support

A professional receptionist greets your clients, answers your calls, and handles your mail — so you look established from day one.

Virtual Office

Get a prestigious Point Cook Town Centre address without a full-time office. Includes mail handling, call answering, and access to meeting rooms as needed.

Casual Hire

Need a desk or room for a day? Casual hire options let you use our facilities without a long-term commitment. Perfect for tradespeople and sole traders.

Kitchen & Amenities

Fully equipped kitchen, individual male, female and accessible bathrooms, and a waiting area for visitors. Everything you'd expect from a corporate environment.
