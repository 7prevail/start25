Our story

A little place big on love

Tucked into the heart of Point Cook's Featherbrook Estate, Little Miss Violet is a cosy neighbourhood cafe serving beautiful brunch, specialty coffee with stunning latte art, and a unique Korean fusion menu that keeps locals coming back.

Kid-friendly, dog-friendly, and bursting with warm hospitality. This is your spot to slow down, indulge, and leave with a big smile.
